About The Event

ASIET Hacks is an amazing opprutunity for students to get a feel for what code is really like.We believe in teaching students to code as coding can be a powerful tool in the hands of a creative genius. Everyone of us has an idea and we want to equip these students with the tools that they'll need to realise their vision. We here at ASIET Hacks believe in inclusion, So even if you are a beginner we'll have something for you that'll help you start your coding journey.
